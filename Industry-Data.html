Multimedia Specialist

Arvee's ideal job

Multimedia specialists make up just 1% of the job listings within the IT industry. This would suggest that it is a niche field with limited job opportunities. To increase job prospects, Arvee could consider a more generalised role that would allow him to work creatively and incorporate multimedia elements such as graphic design or web design.

Full Stack Developer

Bridget and Nikhil's ideal job

Developer roles make up 29% of all the job postings from 2017-2018. Of this 29% just 2% of those job listings are for Full Stack Developers which suggests this may be a difficult field to get into. However, Full Stack Developers can also fulfil the roles of front or backend developers, if Bridget/Nik expanded their job search for these fields their job prospects would increase by over 150%.

IT Support

Renee and Idris' ideal job

With close to ten thousand job postings, it is evident that IT Support roles are in high demand. In fact, Help Desk Officer was the fifth highest advertised role within IT.
